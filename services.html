Logo Design

My logo design package delivers precisely as promised. Following an introductory call, I'll need a detailed brief and a moodboard from you. With these in hand, I'll embark on an in-depth analysis of your brand before starting my creative process. This approach will result in multiple initial concepts, the refinement of these ideas, and ultimately, the presentation of my proposed final logo design, which will be subject to your feedback.

This package includes:

  • Primary logo design
  • Secondary logo design(s)
  • Raw files + exports

Brand Design

This package kicks off in the same vein as my logo design service, but introduces a vital stage focused on the integration of the logo within your complete brand identity. Upon your approval of the logo and the broader visual identity, you will be provided with an exhaustive brand guidelines document. This document is filled with crucial insights and instructions designed to ensure the consistent and cohesive presentation of your brand across all platforms.

This package includes:

  • Logo design package
  • Brand guidelines (.pdf)
  • Typography pairing, colour palettes + more
  • Raw files + exports

UX/UI Design

This package goes a step further by conducting a detailed evaluation of your website, aiming to perfectly align it with your brand’s core identity and values. Whether it involves optimizing your current site or crafting a brand-new web presence from the ground up, my mission is to ensure that your digital representation is a true extension of your brand. This comprehensive approach guarantees that the aesthetics and messaging of your brand are consistently mirrored across your website, providing a cohesive and engaging user experience that stands out in the digital landscape.

This package includes:

  • Brand design Package + Brand guidelines (.pdf)
  • Web design (Figma +/or Webflow)
  • Raw files + exports
